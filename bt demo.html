Silver Oak Group of Institutes consists of two engineering colleges namely, Silver Oak College of Engineering & Technology (more commonly known as SOCET) established in year 2009. Aditya Silver Oak Institute of Technology (more commonly known as ASOIT) established in year 2014. Both institutes are AICTE Approved.
